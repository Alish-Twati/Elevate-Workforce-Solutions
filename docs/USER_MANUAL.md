## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to: `http://localhost/elevate-workforce-solutions/`
3.  You'll see the homepage with featured jobs

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Register** in the navigation menu
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Select user type (Job Seeker or Company)
   - Enter your details
   - Create a strong password
3. Click **Create Account**
4. You'll be redirected to the login page

### Logging In

1.  Click **Login** in the navigation menu
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Login**
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## Job Seeker Guide

### 1.  Browsing Jobs

**From Homepage:**
- View featured jobs on the homepage
- Click "Browse Jobs" to see all available positions

**Search and Filter:**
1. Navigate to "Browse Jobs"
2. Use the search bar to enter keywords
3. Apply filters:
   - Category
   - Job Type (Full-time, Part-time, etc.)
   - Location
   - Experience Level
4. Click **Search**

### 2. Viewing Job Details

1. Click on any job title or "View Details"
2. You'll see:
   - Complete job description
   - Requirements
   - Company information
   - Salary range (if provided)
   - Application deadline
3. Review all details carefully before applying

### 3. Applying for Jobs

**Prerequisites:**
- Resume in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format (max 5MB)
- Cover letter (optional but recommended)

**Application Steps:**
1. Click **Apply Now** on the job details page
2. Write a cover letter (optional)
3. Upload your resume
4. Review your application
5. Click **Submit Application**
6. You'll receive a confirmation message

**Note:** You can only apply once per job. 

### 4. Managing Applications

**View Your Applications:**
1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. See all your submitted applications with:
   - Job title
   - Company name
   - Application date
   - Current status

**Application Statuses:**
- üü° **Pending**: Application submitted, awaiting review
- üîµ **Reviewed**: Company has reviewed your application
- ‚≠ê **Shortlisted**: You've been shortlisted for interview
- ‚úÖ **Accepted**: Congratulations! You got the job

**View Application Details:**
1. Click **View** on any application
2. See your submitted cover letter and resume
3. Download your resume if needed

**Withdraw Application:**
1. Go to application details
2. Click **Withdraw Application** (only for pending applications)
3. Confirm withdrawal

### 5. Dashboard Features

Your dashboard shows:
- Total applications submitted
- Pending applications
- Shortlisted applications
- Accepted applications
- Recent job openings
- Application history

## Company/Employer Guide

### 1. Setting Up Company Profile

**First Time Setup:**
1. After registration, go to **Company Profile**
2. Fill in company details:
   - Company name
   - Description
   - Industry
   - Location
   - Website
   - Company size
   - Founded year
3. Upload company logo (optional)
4. Click **Save Profile**

**Editing Profile:**
1. Navigate to **Company Profile**
2.  Update any information
3. Upload new logo if needed
4. Click **Save Profile**

### 2.  Posting Jobs

**Create New Job Posting:**
1. Click **Post Job** in navigation
2. Fill in job details:

**Basic Information:**
   - Job title
   - Category
   - Job type
   - Location
   - Experience level

**Job Description:**
   - Detailed description of the role
   - Responsibilities
   - Requirements and qualifications

**Compensation:**
   - Minimum salary (optional)
   - Maximum salary (optional)
   - Application deadline (optional)

3. Choose status:
   - **Active**: Publish immediately
   - **Draft**: Save for later

4. Click **Post Job**

### 3. Managing Job Postings

**View Your Jobs:**
1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. See all your job postings with:
   - Job title
   - Status
   - Number of applications
   - Posted date

**Edit Job:**
1. Click edit icon (pencil) on the job
2.  Modify any details
3. Update status if needed
4. Click **Update Job**

**Delete Job:**
1. Click delete icon (trash) on the job
2. Confirm deletion
3. Job and all associated applications will be removed

**Job Statuses:**
- ‚úÖ **Active**: Visible to job seekers, accepting applications
- ‚ùå **Closed**: Not accepting new applications
- üìù **Draft**: Not visible to job seekers

### 4. Managing Applications

**View Applications:**
1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. See recent applications
3. OR click on a job to see all its applications

**Review Application:**
1. Click on an application
2. View applicant details:
   - Name and contact information
   - Cover letter
   - Resume (downloadable)
3. Update application status:
   - Pending
   - Reviewed
   - Shortlisted
   - Accepted
   - Rejected

**Contact Applicants:**
- Email and phone are visible
- Click email to send message
- Click phone to call

### 5. Dashboard Features

Your dashboard shows:
- Total jobs posted
- Active jobs
- Closed jobs
- Total applications received
- Recent applications
- Application status breakdown

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: Is registration free?**
A: Yes, registration is completely free for both job seekers and companies.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Contact the system administrator or use the "Forgot Password" link (if implemented).

**Q: Can I have both job seeker and company accounts?**
A: Use different email addresses for each account type.

### Job Seeker Questions

**Q: How many jobs can I apply for?**
A: Unlimited!  Apply to as many jobs as you want. 

**Q: Can I edit my application after submitting?**
A: No, applications cannot be edited once submitted.  Make sure to review carefully.

**Q: What file formats are accepted for resumes?**
A: PDF, DOC, and DOCX files up to 5MB.

**Q: How do I know if my application was reviewed?**
A: Check your dashboard for status updates.

**Q: Can I apply for the same job twice?**
A: No, only one application per job is allowed.

### Company Questions

**Q: How many jobs can I post?**
A: Unlimited job postings. 

**Q: Can I edit a job after posting?**
A: Yes, edit anytime from your dashboard.

**Q: How do I contact applicants?**
A: Use the email and phone provided in their application.

**Q: Can I reactivate a closed job?**
A: Yes, edit the job and change status to "Active".

**Q: What happens to applications if I delete a job?**
A: All applications for that job are also deleted.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Is the site mobile-friendly?**
A: Yes, fully responsive on all devices.

**Q: Why can't I upload my resume?**
A: Check file size (<5MB) and format (PDF/DOC/DOCX). 

**Q: I'm getting an error. What should I do?**
A: Contact the administrator with error details.
